Our Story

about us.

Built in Kampala. Built for the world.

Meitav Tech Limited was founded on one belief: every Ugandan deserves access to genuine, world-class technology at fair prices. We started on Wilson Road and grew into one of Kampala's most trusted tech store.

We deal in over 30 of the world's top technology brands — from Apple and Samsung to DJI, Canon, and Logitech — sourcing directly from certified distributors so every customer always gets the real thing.

Our team of passionate tech experts is available every day to help you find the right product for your budget, your workflow, and your lifestyle.

Our Target Clients

At Meitav Tech, we make technology simple, reliable, and accessible. Founded to serve everyday users, creative professionals, and small businesses, our retail store offers a carefully selected range of gadgets, computers, cameras, and accessories from trusted brands.

Whether you're upgrading a workstation, building a custom PC, or choosing the right camera lens, our friendly team provides clear, expert advice so you can buy with confidence.

We believe great service begins with listening. Our staff take time to understand your needs, demonstrate products hands-on, and recommend solutions that balance performance, durability, and value.
